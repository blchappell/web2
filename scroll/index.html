My intent with this project is to improve the readership of a web-based article by building out visual and dynamic elements to enhance the content experience. This particular case study examplifies persistance in design and the impact of the solution in response to a real world problem.

Using an integration of sourced photography, strong typography, and visual elements, the article will be broken down to encourage an understanding of the content.

Jaundice is a critical global health issue among newborns. Jaundice is the #1 reason why newborns are readmitted to hospitals worldwide. About 3 in 5 children have some degree of jaundice. For approximately 18% of babies, the condition is severe and requires treatment. Severe jaundice, when left untreated or ineffectively treated, can lead to severe brain damage, a condition called kernicterus, or death.
